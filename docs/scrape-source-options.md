# Web Scrape Source Options

Target: CRE industry content that answers the 4 knowledge base questions (office vacancy drivers, sector stress, forecasts, JLL positioning).

Minimum requirement: 15 sources from 3+ different sites.

---

## Candidate Sources

### 1. JLL Research
**Site:** jll.com/en/trends-and-insights
**Why:** The target company. Quarterly market outlooks, office/industrial/retail sector reports.
**Firecrawl query:**
```
site:jll.com/en/trends-and-insights office vacancy commercial real estate market outlook 2024
```

### 2. CBRE Insights
**Site:** cbre.com/insights
**Why:** JLL's main competitor. Same research format — good for cross-source synthesis and showing multiple analyst perspectives.
**Firecrawl query:**
```
site:cbre.com/insights office market commercial real estate vacancy trends 2024
```

### 3. Cushman & Wakefield
**Site:** cushmanwakefield.com/en/insights
**Why:** Top-3 CRE firm, publishes accessible market reports including their "Office Stats" series. Adds a third major firm voice.
**Firecrawl query:**
```
site:cushmanwakefield.com/en/insights commercial real estate office market recovery 2024
```

### 4. Bisnow
**Site:** bisnow.com
**Why:** CRE trade news site, free articles, covers market trends, major deals, and executive commentary. Good narrative layer.
**Firecrawl query:**
```
site:bisnow.com commercial real estate office vacancy remote work interest rates 2024
```

### 5. Commercial Observer
**Site:** commercialobserver.com
**Why:** CRE trade publication with well-written articles on market conditions, lending stress, and forecasts.
**Firecrawl query:**
```
site:commercialobserver.com commercial real estate market outlook delinquency office 2024
```
