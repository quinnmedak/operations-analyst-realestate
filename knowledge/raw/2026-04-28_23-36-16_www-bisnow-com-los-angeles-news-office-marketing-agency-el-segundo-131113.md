# Marketing Agency To Vacate Playa Vista For Frank Gehry-Designed Office In El Segundo

## Deal Summary
Media agency Canvas Worldwide expanded into 68,000 SF at 2330 Utah Ave. in El Segundo (Frank Gehry-designed campus), relocating from approximately 36,000 SF in Playa Vista. Roughly doubled its space. JLL Managing Director Jason Fine and Senior Managing Director Blake Searles represented Canvas Worldwide.

Canvas Worldwide clients include Hyundai, Kia, Genesis, Amazon MGM Studios, Edward Jones, McDonald's, and Zillow.

## El Segundo Market Context
El Segundo has seen a steady stream of larger office leases since the start of 2025:
- The Toy Association signed a 95,000 SF lease at 101 Continental
- KPMG signed a 50,000 SF lease at Plaza at Continental Park

El Segundo availability rate in Q2 2025 was 23%, below the marketwide 28.1% rate — showing El Segundo outperforming the broader LA office market.

## Tenant Landscape Insight
Media and marketing agencies (Canvas Worldwide), professional services (KPMG), and trade/industry associations (The Toy Association) are active in the El Segundo/South Bay office submarket. El Segundo's proximity to LAX, entertainment studios, and aerospace employers (aerospace tenants also cluster here) drives demand from diverse industries.
