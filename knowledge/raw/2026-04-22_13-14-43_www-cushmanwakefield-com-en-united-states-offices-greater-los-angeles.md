# Commercial Real Estate in Greater Los Angeles

Los Angeles is known around the globe for its picturesque landscapes, endless sunshine and undisputed standing as the Entertainment Capital of the World.

It’s also the country’s second-most populated metro area and ranks among the world’s largest and most diversified economies thanks to its dense consumer base, deep talent pool, plentiful sources of capital and access to the Ports of Long Beach and Los Angeles, and Los Angeles International Airport (LAX).

Whether you’re looking to start or expand a business here, drive better performance for your real estate holdings, or simply want to understand how global real estate trends can impact your business strategy, we’re here to help.

Cushman & Wakefield has a powerful market presence throughout Southern California, with five offices and more than 150 brokerage professionals covering all submarkets and product types, including [industrial](https://www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/united-states/properties/california/industrial-for-lease-greater-la) and [office](https://www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/united-states/properties/california/office-for-lease-greater-la) space. Our extensive expertise includes a wide range of [commercial properties in California](https://www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/united-states/properties/california), ensuring tailored solutions for every client. Additionally, our clients benefit from market intelligence and insights drawn from 400 additional global offices in 70 countries.
