# Hankey Investment Co., Barker Pacific Group Buy Sunset Media Center For $61M

## Deal Summary
22-story Sunset Media Center (326,000 SF) at 6255 Sunset Blvd., Hollywood sold for $61M to joint venture of Barker Pacific Group and Hankey Investment Co. Previous owner Kilroy Realty had been steadily selling office properties. Property was approximately 51% occupied at time of sale. Tenants include Hollywood Chamber of Commerce, OpenTable, and AIDS Healthcare Foundation.

## Value-Add Thesis
Buyers positioned as value-add / lease-up play at fraction of replacement cost: "The profile of this acquisition perfectly reflects our desire to own well located, high quality office assets with strong in-place yields, at a fraction of replacement cost, with additional cash flow upside through lease-up." — Mark Handin, BPG Managing Principal

## LA Office Market Context
- LA office leasing activity in 2025 hit new pandemic-era and post-pandemic high: **14M+ SF of leasing activity**, up 600K SF over 2024.
- Q4 2025 leasing activity: 3.8M SF (same as Q4 2024)
- Q4 2025 availability: fell 60 bps to 27.6% (Savills)
- Hollywood availability: 31.3%, higher than marketwide rate

## Tenant and Investor Landscape
Entertainment-adjacent tenants (Hollywood Chamber of Commerce), tech/services (OpenTable), healthcare (AIDS Healthcare Foundation) occupy Hollywood office space. Kilroy Realty — major LA office REIT — actively shedding assets as it repositions portfolio. Private capital seeing value-add opportunities in half-vacant towers at distressed pricing.
